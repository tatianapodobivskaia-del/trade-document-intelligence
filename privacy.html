Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 22, 2026

1. Overview

TradeScreenAI ("we," "our," or "the Service") is an AI-powered sanctions screening research prototype developed by Tatiana Podobivskaia. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our Service at tradescreenai.com.

2. Information We Collect

Data You Provide: When you use the screening tool, you may input vendor names, countries, transaction amounts, document types, and Cyrillic text. This data is processed in real-time and is not permanently stored on our servers.

Automatically Collected: We may collect standard web analytics data including IP addresses, browser type, pages visited, and timestamps.

3. How We Use Information

  • To perform sanctions screening against publicly available sanctions lists (OFAC SDN, EU, UN, UK OFSI)
  • To provide AI-powered risk analysis via Azure OpenAI
  • To improve the accuracy and performance of our screening algorithms
  • To generate compliance reports during your session

4. Data Processing and Storage

Vendor data submitted for screening is processed in real-time via Azure Cloud services (East US region). We do not permanently store your vendor data or screening results after your session ends. Session data exists only in your browser's memory.

5. Third-Party Services

  • Microsoft Azure: Cloud computing and AI services. Subject to Microsoft's Privacy Statement.
  • Vercel: Website hosting. Subject to Vercel's Privacy Policy.
  • U.S. Treasury, EU, UN, UK Government: Publicly available sanctions data.

6. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures including HTTPS encryption for all data in transit. No method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights

Since we do not permanently store personal data from screening sessions, there is generally no personal data to access, correct, or delete. If you have questions, contact us below.

8. Children's Privacy

TradeScreenAI is not intended for use by individuals under 18 years of age.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
